# SuperStore Sales Analysis

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** built using the SuperStore dataset. 
The dashboard provides business insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.

The goal of this project is to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify profitable products and customers, evaluate regional performance, and support data-driven decision making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Track profit trends over time.
- Identify the best-selling products.
- Compare sales across categories.
- Evaluate regional performance.
- Discover the most valuable customers.
- Monitor profitability by customer segment.
- Analyze shipping mode performance.

---

## Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop**
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Visualization

# Dashboard Analysis

## 1. Overall Business Performance

The company generated:

- **$2.33 Million** in sales
- **$292.3K** profit
- **5,111** orders
- **12.56%** profit margin

Although revenue is high, the profit margin suggests there is still room to improve profitability by reducing costs or improving pricing strategies.

---

## 2. Sales by Category

| Category | Sales |
|-----------|--------|
| Technology | **$839.89K** |
| Furniture | **$754.75K** |
| Office Supplies | **$731.89K** |

### Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing category.
- Furniture contributes significantly but is often associated with lower margins in SuperStore datasets.
- Office Supplies provides consistent revenue.

---

## 3. Regional Performance

The dashboard compares sales and profit across four regions.

### Insights

- West generates the highest revenue and profit.
- South contributes the least.
- Regional performance indicates opportunities to improve sales strategies in lower-performing regions.

---

## 4. Monthly Profit Trend

The dashboard tracks monthly profit from **2023 to 2026**.

### Insights

- Profit fluctuates throughout the years.
- Several seasonal peaks suggest periods of strong customer demand.
- Business performance is not consistent month-to-month, highlighting the need for better forecasting and inventory planning.

---

## 5. Top Selling Products

Highest revenue-generating products include:

- Canon ImageClass
- Fellowes Products
- Cisco TelePresence
- HON Office Furniture
- GBC Binding Products

### Insights

These products contribute significantly to overall revenue and should remain a focus for inventory management and marketing.

---

## 7. Customer Segment Performance

Segments analyzed:

- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

### Insights

- Consumer segment generates the highest profit.
- Corporate follows closely.
- Home Office contributes the least.

This suggests that marketing campaigns targeting individual consumers produce the highest return.

---

## 8. Sales by Ship Mode

Shipping methods include:

- Standard Class
- Second Class
- First Class
- Same Day

### Insights

Standard Class accounts for the majority of sales, indicating that customers prioritize cost-effective shipping over speed.

---

## 9. Geographic Analysis

### Country

Nearly all profits originate from the **United States**, while **Canada** contributes only a small share.

### City Performance

Top-performing cities include:

- New York
- Los Angeles
- Seattle
- San Francisco
- Detroit

These cities represent key revenue markets and should remain strategic priorities.

---

# Key Business Insights

1. Technology is the strongest product category.

2. The West region consistently outperforms all other regions.

3. Consumer customers generate the highest profits.

4. Standard Class is the preferred shipping method.

5. A small number of customers account for a significant portion of total sales.

6. Profit margins remain relatively modest despite strong revenue, suggesting opportunities to optimize discounts, pricing, or operational costs.

---

# Recommendations

- Increase investment in Technology products due to their strong sales performance.
- Expand marketing efforts in the South region to boost underperforming sales.
- Develop customer loyalty programs for top-value customers.
- Review pricing and discount strategies to improve profit margins.
- Maintain adequate inventory levels for top-selling products.
- Investigate low-performing product categories and cities for improvement opportunities.
- Use seasonal profit trends to optimize inventory and promotional campaigns.
